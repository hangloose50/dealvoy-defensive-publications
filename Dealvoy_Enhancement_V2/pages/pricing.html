Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features.

What's included in the free plan?

The free Starter plan includes 5 core AI agents, basic deal discovery, community support, mobile app access, and 100 product scans per month. It's perfect for getting started and testing our platform.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

What's the difference between the AI agents?

Each AI agent specializes in different aspects of e-commerce automation. Higher tier plans include more sophisticated agents for advanced features like custom integrations, automated repricing, and market intelligence.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise customers receive white-glove onboarding with no additional setup costs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full.

Need a Custom Solution?

Large teams and enterprises need custom solutions. Let's discuss your specific requirements and create a plan that fits your business perfectly.
